Our Classes

Enjoy classes with some of the best lindy hop instructors in the world, Skye Humphries and Naomi Uyama!

CLASS SETUP

We will have a single track of classes open to dancers that meet or exceed the prerequisite skill level (intermediate). There will not be an audition process; however, the instructors will not stop or explain content covered by the prerequisites. Please be respectful of other dancers and sign up only if you meet the requirements.

For dancers that feel they exceed the requirements, please still consider attending classes. Skye and Naomi are incredible instructors that bring valuable content to all levels. You won't be disappointed!

PREREQUISITES

Please register for classes only if you meet or exceed the following requirements. We define "feeling comfortable" as being able to execute these movements in your social dancing.

I feel comfortable dancing (a):

  • swingout (from closed, from open, back to closed)
  • basic 6-count
  • pass by
  • tuck turn
  • side-by-side charleston
  • hand-to-hand charleston
  • tandem charleston

CLASS PASSES

This year, we will be offering two types of class pass: the All-Access Weekend Pass and the Short Weekend Pass. Most of the class passes will be All-Access Weekend Pass, which will get you into any of the scheduled activities, except competitions. We will also have a small number of Short Weekend Passes, which will get you into SATURDAY classes and all the dances. Select this option if you'd like to spend Sunday traveling home.

See class pass pricing.

VENUE DESCRIPTION

Classes are held at the Overture Center for the Arts, a beautiful venue close to parking, lunch, and shopping. All classes will be held in rooms with well cared for hardwood floors. There are drinking fountains outside the classroom, so we recommend bringing a reusable water bottle (with a lid) if you want water in the room.
