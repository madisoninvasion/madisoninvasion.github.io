Not from Madison? Looking for a place to stay? Once you have registered for Madison Invasion, you are all set to select your housing option.

LOCAL HOUSING

Some dancers from Jumptown have graciously offered to host out-of-towners! Housing requests can be made using this form. We'll do our best to pair you with a great local host!

If you are local and have some extra space in your abode, let us know whether you are interesting in hosting dancers. Please complete the housing form to sign up as a host!

OTHER HOUSING

This year, our housing coordinator is posting links to other housing options on our Facebook event page. If you want AirBnB or hostel recommendations, check there!
