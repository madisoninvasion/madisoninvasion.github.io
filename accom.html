Our Accommodations

Not from Madison? Looking for a place to stay? Use this form to request housing with a local or check out our hotel recommendations.

LOCAL HOUSING

Some dancers from Jumptown have graciously offered to host out-of-towners! Please submit your housing request as early as possible, as there are a limited number of spaces available. We will do our best to find a place for you to stay.

If you are local and have some extra space in your abode, let us know whether you are interesting in hosting dancers. Please complete the housing form to sign up as a host!

HOTELS

We are currently looking for a special hotel to host our guests. Please check back for more information

QUESTIONS

If you have any questions specific to housing, please contact our housing coordinator, Charity, or the Madison Invasion team.
