Our Accommodations

Are you coming to Madison Invasion from out of town and looking for a place to stay? Once you’ve completed your registration for Invasion, you’re set to select accommodations from a variety of housing options!

HOTEL

Jumptown has reserved a block of rooms at the Hyatt Place Madison/Downtown and the Howard Johnson Plaza Hotel with discounted rates especially for Invasion attendees. See below for more information.

Hyatt Place Madison/Downtown

The Hyatt is conveniently located in the heart of downtown. It's just 4 minutes down the road to the evening venue (The Discovery Center) and 8 minutes to get to classes. And with plenty of restaurant choices right next door, it's a great place to call home for the weeked. You can choose to book online or by calling the hotel directly, and be sure to mention the keywords for the discount: Madison Invasion 2020

Online booking
Nightly Rate: $129
Phone Number: 1.888.492.8847
Hotel Address: 333 W Washington, Madison, WI 53703

Howard Johnson Plaza Hotel

The Howard Johnson Hotel is a little further on the East side of town, but still just an 8 minute drive to our class venue (The Madison Circus Space) and 15 minutes to the Discovery Center and downtown. Bookings can be made by calling the hotel; mention "Madison Invasion" to get included in our group!

Nightly Rate: $90
Phone Number: 608.819.6298
Hotel Address: 3841 E Washington, Madison, WI 53704

HOSTEL

HI-Hostel Madison

A great inexpensive option is the vibrant HI-Hostel, located just ½ mile from the Overture Center. The Hostel offers both Private and Dorm-style (shared) rooms with a shared bathroom facility, free linens/towels, a fully equipped guest kitchen, common room with library/television/VCR/internet kiosk, and laundry facility – as well as Free Wi-Fi and a Free Continental Breakfast! Limited on-site is parking available. Call or email for a reservation.

Phone Number: 1.608.441.0144
Email: madison@hiusa.org
Hotel Address: 141 S. Butler Street, Madison, WI 53703

LOCAL HOUSING

Some hospitable Jumptown dancers have graciously offered to host out-of-towners in their homes! There are a limited number of spaces available, so we can't guarantee spots for everyone. We will do our best to match you with a compatible host and a comfortable and convenient place for you to stay! When you register you will have an option to request local housing. Housing requests will be accepted until March 13th.

Madisonians/Jumptowners – if you have an extra room or other space available and are interested in hosting, please select "Yes, I would be willing to host a dancer or musician" when you register.

If you have already registered and would like to modify your choice (either to host or to request housing), please contact us.

QUESTIONS

If you have any questions specific to housing, please contact our housing coordinator, Charity, or the Madison Invasion team.
