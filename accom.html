Our Accommodations

Are you coming to Madison Invasion from out of town and looking for a place to stay? Once you’ve completed your registration for Invasion, you’re set to select accommodations from a variety of housing options!

HOTEL

The Madison Concourse Hotel

Jumptown has reserved a block of rooms at the Madison Concourse Hotel with a discounted rate especially for Invasion attendees. The Concourse is conveniently located only ONE BLOCK away from our Saturday/Sunday venue. You can literally roll out of bed, stop at the Starbucks (located in the building), and get to classes within 15 minutes! You can choose to book online or by calling the hotel directly, and be sure to mention the keywords for the discount: The Madison Invasion 2018

Online booking
Phone Number: 1.800.356.8293
Hotel Address: 1 West Dayton St, Madison, WI 53703

HOSTEL

HI-Hostel Madison

A great inexpensive option is the vibrant HI-Hostel, located just ½ mile from the Overture Center. The Hostel offers both Private and Dorm-style (shared) rooms with a shared bathroom facility, free linens/towels, a fully equipped guest kitchen, common room with library/television/VCR/internet kiosk, and laundry facility – as well as Free Wi-Fi and a Free Continental Breakfast! Limited on-site is parking available. Call or email for a reservation.

Phone Number: 1.608.441.0144
Email: madison@hiusa.org
Hotel Address: 141 S. Butler Street, Madison, WI 53703

LOCAL HOUSING

Some hospitable Jumptown dancers have graciously offered to host out-of-towners in their homes! There are a limited number of spaces available, so please submit your housing request as soon as possible. We will do our best to match you with a compatible host and a comfortable and convenient place for you to stay! Housing requests can be made using this form. Housing requests will be accepted until March 14th.

Madisonians/Jumptowners – if you have an extra room or other space available and are interested in hosting, please complete the housing form to sign up as a host!

OTHER HOUSING

This year, our housing coordinator is posting links to other housing options on our Facebook event page. If you want AirBnB recommendations, check there!

QUESTIONS

If you have any questions specific to housing, please contact our housing coordinator, Charity, or the Madison Invasion team.
