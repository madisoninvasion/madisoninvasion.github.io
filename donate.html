Our Donations

Thank you very much for interest in donating to Jumptown’s The Madison Invasion.

Jumptown

Jumptown is one of Madison’s premiere community organizations for swing dancing. What started out as a student organization at the University of Wisconsin - Madison over 12 years ago grew into a non-profit that offers weekly classes and social dances that serves approximately 50 dancers (and growing) across Madison and neighboring cities. We are proud to share our love of authentic, swing-era Lindy Hop with a vibrant, dedicated, and talented community of dancers.

The Madison Invasion

The Madison Invasion is a weekend-long swing dancing workshop. We are bringing in world-class instructors, offering 8 hours of instruction, and hosting two social dances with live music. This is a tremendous opportunity for our scene to spread the joy of our dance and share beauty of our city with swing dancers across the Midwest. Moreover, this a fabulous opportunity for all dancers to learn movement and techniques from master instructors and build their skills and styles with new people.

It is Jumptown’s goal to make The Madison Invasion as accessible and affordable as possible. We do not profit from this event and aim to keep the cost down so that more people could attend. Your donations would enable that to happen and continue this great tradition of weekend learning in our swing dancing community.

Ways to Donate

  • Make a direct donation: Any amount you wish! Every dollar counts!
  • Sponsor a dancer: For $150, you can sponsor a dancer. That money will cover the cost of an All-Access pass to attend all Invasion events and cover some organization expenses associated with attendance. We are looking to sponsor dancers in the following categories:
    • First-time dancers
    • Dancers who travel great distances to participate
    • Dancers who need financial support
  • Advertise your business: We have opportunities for advertising. Your logo would be featured on our website. We have over 300 pageviews on our website to date and anticipate that number significantly rising as the event approaches.
  • Share your services: You may share coupons for your business to gain patronage from dancers attending the event, or donate meals for our instructors or for our late-night dance event to offset the cost of food.

Ready to Donate?
