Our FAQ

If you have any questions that you can't find in FAQ, please contact us.

I didn't get a confirmation from the event that my PayPal payment went through. Did it?

After you receive confirmation from PayPal for your payment, you can click the booking link on the thank you page or from your registration email to review your booking and confirm there is no outstanding balance on your account.

We have not gotten the system to consistently send payment confirmation after PayPal accepts payment. Despite this, it is tracking it all in our system. If you haven't gotten a confirmation email (from The Madison Invasion), can't access your booking via your registration email, and want to be extra sure that we got your payment, please email us and we can check.

Can I get a refund if I can't make it?

Sorry! Tickets are non-refundable! You can transfer your ticket to someone else, with some restrictions. Please contact us for more details. The sooner the better so we can make sure everyone gets what they need!

Will there be early bird pricing?

Nope, each of the pass types will cost the same amount no matter when you purchase them. That is, unless we sell out! So getting in there early is still in your best interest.

Will there be any competitions?

Not this year. We would like this event to be focused on learning, comradery, and social dancing. This will also allow us more time to show off local and regional performances. If you are interested in performing an exhibition routine, please contact us!

Can I purchase entry to just one dance? Can I buy tickets at the event?

Yes! Tickets for individual dances will be available at the door. Tickets for individual classes may also be available at the door, however we may sell out ahead of the event, so your best bet is to book one of our pass options.
