Our Venues

Discovery Building (The Wisconsin Institutes for Discovery)
330 N. Orchard St.

The Discovery Building is a new building on the University of Wisconsin campus. Both our Friday night and Saturday night dances will be held in the H.F. DeLuca Forum, located in the heart of Town Center, the first floor of the building. With such a beautiful hardwood floor, you won't want to stop dancing, but there is plenty of seating outside the room to allow you to relax and catch up with friends if you need a break.

Madison Circus Space
2082 Winnebago St.

The Madison Circus Space has recently built a brand new space just one block away from Atwood Avenue, the heart of the Atwood-Schenk neighborhood. All of the Saturday and Sunday classes will be held at this venue, in their two dance studio spaces.

Nearby Parking

Downtown Madison has plenty of reasonably priced parking options. Several University parking ramps are available near the Discovery Building.

For the Friday and Saturday night dances, parking in the ramps will likely be the easiest option. Click on the lot links to find more information, including a real-time listing of spaces available. You may also consult the Discovery Building Parking page for more details. If you would prefer street parking, try the nearby neighborhoods to the south and southeast of the building.

The Madison Circus Space has a small dedicated lot, however it will likely fill up quickly. There are street parking options along Winnebago and generally much more availability along the side streets off of Bashford Ave.
