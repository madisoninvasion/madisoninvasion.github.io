Our Competitions

Our events have always been about having fun! Our competitions are no different. Come on out and show us what you've got!

NOVICE LUCK OF THE DRAW

We love to support our newer dancers! If you are new to competition and you do not have a partner, this event is for you! During the preliminary round, you will be randomly paired with 3 partners to dance your heart out. Judges will select which dancers they want to see more from, then those dancers will be randomly paired for the final round. We will do spotlights for couples and judges will select the best! It's a great way to show off your social dance skills and get started in competitions. Sign up on the registration form or on Friday night!

ELIGIBILITY

You are eligible to participate if you have never placed in a Novice or Amateur Luck of the Draw finals (or similar).

ENTRY FEE

$5

OPEN LUCK OF THE DRAW

This competition for our more experienced dancers. The same rules apply as in the Novice Luck of the Draw, but geared toward dancers that are no longer eligible for our novice level.

ELIGIBILITY

Anyone not signed up for the Novice Luck of the Draw. More specifically, those that have placed in a Novice or Amateur Luck of the Draw (or beyond!) in the past.

ENTRY FEE

$5
